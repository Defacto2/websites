 . So you're trying to figure out why I am giving away money for speed? To  .
   tell you the truth, I became a cop because I loved the idea of " owning
   the road ", having high-speed chases, taking on tight curves! I loved it!
   But the, reality of the job was that I spent all of my time handing out
   tickets. Citizens were so scared of the law, that no one ever gave me a
   real " run for my money! ". One day, I was given the key to changing my
   destiny when I won one million dollars in the lottery! That's when I
   decided that the time for racers had arrived!

       The roads are meant to be controlled by drivers, their choice, their
   speed! I set up a series of strategically located radar guns that I will
   use to record racers' speed as they fly by and reward the fastest drivers
   with cold hard cash! And the best part is that drivers can upgrade their
   cars and make them fly faster or simply add a bit of your own personal
   style!

   At the Beggining :

    In the beginning stages of Speed Busters, the team began the project
   by observing what had been achieved in racing games in the past, then
   selected elements that brought gamers the most fun. They tried to bring
   back some of the management aspects of the old 16-bit racing games
   like Sprint Rally and others.

    The team wanted to deliver a true feeling of speeding, a feeling of
   ownership, the need for driving strategy, and surprises like jumping on a
   train that is running and using it as a shortcut, avoiding an avalanche
   crashing in front of you, or being chased by water in an irrigation
   tunnel. Add this to the strategy of upgrading your car with money earned
   from speeding.

    Once the initial elements were finalized, the creation teams split into
   three specialized groups all working together to make the dream of Speed
   Busters come alive, comprised of Designers, Graphists and Programmers.


   Cars :       

    Seven cars have been specially designed based on models from the 1950s
   era and the 1990s era. All the cars sustain damage in 12 different spots
   at three levels of severity and the more damage you have the more your
   car's appearance and performance will be affected. Speed Busters:
   American Highways also offers players the opportunity to upgrade or buy
   new cars as they win cash blasting through the radar!

    Check out the cars for yourself and you will see some of the unique
   features each offers in terms of performance and handling.

   Tracks :    

    Feel the wind in your hair as you cruise on six main tracks plus one
   hidden track, all based on real North American locations. With 21 track
   configurations and four different me-of-day and weather conditions,
   including rain, snow, day and dusk, the ride is never the same! The
   track surface has also been designed to push drivers to the limit,
   featuring eight different terrain surfaces!

    Take a look at the tracks for yourself and you will see some of the
   unique features each track has to offer drivers.
