ChinaBlue and the Truth
by DToxR

Coverage Overview

  Why in the world would a well respected member of the warez scene decide to quit cold turkey for no apparent reason? That question was on a lot of peoples' minds when ChinaBlue announced her retirement from the scene this past month. Citing various forms of harassment as the main reason in a letter intended to shed some light on the subject, ChinaBlue went on to describe a series of anonymous phone calls and postings - the end result being the cancellation of her ISP accounts and her personal information being plastered on the 'net for all to see. In a "line of work" where anonymity is key, this type of attack can be devastating. The source of these attacks is unknown, although when asked, ChinaBlue is "99% sure" that the culprit is Stingray, an active member of X-Force who was unavailable for comment for this feature. Logs were provided from a conversation in which "Stingray" accuses CB of being a narc. As an interesting twist to this story, scenelink was contacted by RoAMER, a staff member of Beyond Akira, which ChineBlue had been accused of narcing. RoAMER went on to personally clear her from any involvement in the board's demise. The damage has already been done however, and it has cost the scene a valuable member and good friend. As for who is to blame, that is open to speculation; ChinaBlue would be the first to discourage starting any more fires under what is basically a done deal. In business that's all about trickle down, suppliers like ChinaBlue are the source and the rest of us reap the rewards.
