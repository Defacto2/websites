coop groups term.
1. Slang for "cooperating groups" 2. Groups that help make scenelink possible by agreeing to send in info on themselves for the scene index. 3. Groups that are making sure scenelink remains a cooperative effort instead of one group dictating everything.

issuepage.
1. What we loosely call the biweekly updating we do for the website. Every two weeks you will find new articles, index entries, contests, and announcements from groups. Other projects are updated at different times, see the site map for more info.

listservemail.
1. A weekly emailing that goes to those who request it. 2. One of several weekly mailings scenelink does to keep people informed about what's going on: I'm on the coop group listserv, and I just added myself to the general listserv as well.

membershipthingy.
1. NOT to be confused with being a coop group (open to anyone), membership to the group scenelink is for individuals and is moderately selective.

scenelinkgroup.
1. The group of projects trying to help the scenes with cooperation from many scene groups. 2. The group of individuals who are coordinating this effort. 3. The website where these projects are located.

sitemappage.
1. A page that summarizes what's on scenelink.org and how often it's updated.
