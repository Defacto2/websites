Each month Sodom will be giving an award for the best of person in the following categories:

  • Supplier
  • Cracker
  • Packager
  • Courier
These awards will be based on both quantity and quality. The winner will be announced on this page, in the Sodom newsblock and our nfo.

At the end of the year we will tally up the total number awards for each month and catagory. The person who has received the most awards for the year in their catagory will be given the award for supplier, cracker, packager, or courier of the year. They will also win a prize for their award which will be provided by the founder of Sodom, Saint-Tok.
