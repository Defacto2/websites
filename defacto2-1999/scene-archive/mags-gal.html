Galactic Review by Galactic Review (1991)

There is little known about this coded review magazine except for it was probably the first coded PC game review for our scene. The review used a cheesy 1991 (plasma etc.) VGA interface and contained reviews of the games released recently at the time. The reviews included a couple of paragraphs of text about the game and a four item scoring system that was based out of ten. The reviews also contain specific information on the releases such as by who and when the games where release as well as the odd screenshot too.
