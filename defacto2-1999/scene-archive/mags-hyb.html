Hybrid Christmas Magazine by Hybrid (1996)

Something that one should not take too seriously as it was released just for some fun. It does though have some interesting articles and interviews plus a vote of the best groups of 1996. A sequal was ment to appear for the 1997 christmas but it never came to be. For Windows 9x Only.
