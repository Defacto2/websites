Lancelot 2 by Sharp (1992)

Lancelot was originally a emag which was managed by The Dream Team and TRSI back in 1991. But in Decemeber of that year it died. In late 1992 Shape (A TDT/ACiD member) restarted the magazine. Lancelot 2 mainly focuses on the cracking scene of the time, but unfortunately the number of articles and the like are lacking at times.
