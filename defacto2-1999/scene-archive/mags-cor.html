Corruption by Corruption Magazine (1993-4)

Corruption was originally a ansi magazine that was founded by some ACID members. The magazine was intended to be released every second month and survived for four issues. Unlike most magazines Corruption focused on all underground scenes including ansi, hpav, and cracking.
