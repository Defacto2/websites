  Defacto 2 - About Us 

Defacto 2 is a program designed to support underground (but legal) groups and webpages out there in the world known as the internet. Our program is design to unite groups under a common and recognisable name to give those numerous but small groups the power of a major player. This enables the smaller member groups (called divisions or affiliations) to use a united brand name of Defacto 2 for their releases and products.

While all the divisions are running independently of each other, they each have access to shared resources which is available to all divisional groups. Using these resources and a single name means that you don’t have to spend time worrying about establishing a name for yourselves, nor do you have to focus your efforts into public relations which could be better spent elsewhere.

Obviously with so many different groups and affiliates using the same resources there has to be some agreement onto how these are managed. And Defacto 2 is no exception, for there to be no disagreement we have a clear and concessive set of rules (listed in a constitution) that must be followed. While this may seem very authoritarian at first, upon reading the constitution you will find that the rules do not interfere with the running of the individual divisions.
 

  Defacto 2 - Joining 

After reading our constitution, if you feel that your group, webpage etc. would like to join Defacto 2 or you would like to start up a new division then submit a proposal to ipggi@defacto2.net. Your proposal should include who, what, how, why. Plus anything else you think would attract yourselves away from the rest of the crowd. We are pretty selective over who we choose, but don’t let that stop you from trying, we like originality.

If you are wishing to apply or to contact a Defacto 2 division or affiliate please go to that group's webpage.
