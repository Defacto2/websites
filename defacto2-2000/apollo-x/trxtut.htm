This is a small tutorial on how to make cool computer music,using just your,
imagination,computer and a tracking program.It's called tracking,that is because you
put the different instruments and samples in tracks,and the combined tracks make the
song.Samples can be anything,for example an old lady screaming or something...

The tutorial is mainly done by Matthew Coulson (Cools),but I (biXen) will add more
tutorials and update this page frequently,the other submissions will also be credited.
I will also get other trackers to add some tricks here,so don't give up just yet !

Remember...Your best tool is your imagination...and DON'T give up,
hard practice gives results,always...I know it !!!

Intermediate Stadium-If you know the basics,and have done some tracking
Advanced Stadium-If you are quite experienced with a tracker,and want more
