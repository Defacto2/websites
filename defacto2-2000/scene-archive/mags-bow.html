Brotherhood of Warez (1994 & 1999)

An attempt at a not so serious magazine with a theme that parodies the crap parts of the kiddie warez scene. While this makes it fun to look at it also makes it a bitch to read, one can almost think they went a bit too far. But maybe that was the point? Story wise they cover a variety of issues of the nine editions including all the stuff you would expect to find in a magazine like this.
