Inquisition (1995/6)

Inquisition started out as WWN with a different name. Though over time INQ held it's own developing it own style away from the now deflunked WWN. Ironically INQ died the same way as WWN with no one bothering to work on it after issue 11. INQ was the last magazine (that was released on a regular basis) to be managed and edited by prominent scene members. This in turn gave INQ the unwanted reputation for being very bias towards some groups and even more bias against others.
