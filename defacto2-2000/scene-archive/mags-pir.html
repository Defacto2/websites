Pirate (1989-1990)

Pirate is an early text magazine that primarily focused on the IBM PC. It is easy to see they where highly influenced by the hacking magazines of the time and didn't go to far their own way. This is proven by the fact that there are more articles on hacking and viruses in some issues then pirating. Also one might note that many of the articles are not original but reprinted items from previously release text files and main stream news items.
