Cracking 101 (1990)

A four part tutorial that goes into the how and what of cracking software for the IBM PC in the late eighties early nineties. While this tutorial is completely irrelevant today seeing DOS, and it's software is long since dead. It does make an interesting read for those who have an interest in these kinds of things.
