Anti Warez Association (1993)

These guys did the scene thing, got bitten and wanted some payback. So AWA was created in a bid to enlighten the mis-guided souls who remained active in the bad thing called warez. If they couldn't join in the action anymore (for whatever reasons) they sure as hell where going to get back at all those who bought them downwards.

So what you endup is three issues of very humorous articles that make fun of your stereotypical 'warez kid'. Fortunately like all stereotypes they are not true for every one, unfortunately I bet you do know at least someone who could be described perfectly in one of these issues.
