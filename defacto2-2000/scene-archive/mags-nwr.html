The Net Monkey Weekly Report (1998/Still Alive)

Founded in early 1998, Netmonkey Weekend Report as it originally was called (hence N.W.R.) was formed as scoresheet for the courier scene. The N.W.R. founders thought the competition such as the C.W.S. was not up to scratch and so they created their own report. This report quickly evolved into a regularly released text based magazine with thorough game and utility reviews, plus group and video reviews. Also there are usually a couple of interviews or articles thrown in too for good measure.

N.W.R. is highly influenced by previous text based magazines especially by Inquisition. Which is not surprising seeing as one of the leaders of Inquisition is also the leader of N.W.R. N.W.R. can be best described as a tabloid style text magazine that doesn't take itself too seriously. But it does make a fun read and can be quite extensive.
