Founded: 1994 (PC Division)
    Died: February 1998
    Returned: 30th June 1998

    MOTiV8 was born 6 years ago in the Commodore 64 scene as a demo and release group. Mason a member of the M8 C64 later in 1994, founded with Xerox the MOTiV8 PC Section on the internet. MOTiV8 at the beginning was a ftp anonymous courier group, then in March 1995, MOTiV8 started to support private sites. Later we got our first net WHQ, DeathStar. MOTiV8 is a courier group but in the past we released "for fun" only many times, some games and utils, with the help of Mason and other councils of motiv8.

    Motiv8 history taken from the Motiv8 webpage.
