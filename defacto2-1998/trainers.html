Welcome to the new and improved pages filled with stuff from the trainer scene. These pages are dedicated to the scene as it was before 1996 and thus you won't find any trainers for new games. If you are looking for a trainer to that favourite game you just dug up from some old stream tape or disk some place then is the right place for you! Or if you just feel like checking some of the nfo's in the archives or intros that might be in them this is a good place to look.

    Jammer

2000 AD was the first real group I (Jammer) joined. Not soon after joining I started working on a trainer engine. First game we trained was Chaos Engine and I think it was a +6 trainer or something. Shortly after we got around making realmode trainers Replicator and Leatherman created the worlds first protected mode trainer (Jazz Jackrabbit). It was a byte patch trainer but still it did the job. Now we got really busy training pmode games since noone else did! Sad to say I lost my complete 2000 AD collection of trainers and other code so what you see here is the little I have left. Please send me more if you have any not litsted here!
